# **Hour Registration for KLM Projects**

### **Purpose**

This policy outlines the procedure for hourly registration for all employees working on KLM projects. Adhering to this policy is critical for maintaining transparency, accuracy in billing, and strengthening our partnership with KLM by meeting the set KPIs for timesheets. 

### KLM Weekly Timesheet Submission

- In addition to the Sytac monthly timesheet, employees working on KLM projects must also fill out a KLM-specific timesheet every week
- The KLM timesheet must be completed and submitted by the end of the day every Friday
- This weekly submission is crucial for maintaining the partnership with KLM and achieving the set KPIs.

### Sytac Timesheet Submission (unchanged)

- All employees must continue to complete and submit the [Sytac timesheet](https://www.notion.so/Hour-Registration-cb13a6a7602240dca26eba75a4da0edc?pvs=21) on a monthly basis
- Submit your timesheet by the end of each calendar month, and no later than the 5th working day of the following month
