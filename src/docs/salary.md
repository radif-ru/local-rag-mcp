# Salary Payment

Salaries are paid **monthly in arrears** via bank transfer to the bank account designated by the employee.

Payment is **typically made by the 25th of each month**, but may occur later. In all cases, salary will be paid **no later than the last calendar day of the month**.

Please ensure any changes to your bank account details are updated in **BambooHR** before the payment date.

If you have any questions about your salary payment, please email **billing@sytac.io**.
