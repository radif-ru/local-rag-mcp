# Laptop Policy

As developers, two essential tools enable us to make great things happen: our brains and computers. While we have already started many initiatives to keep your knowledge sharp and up to date, it's time to look at how we manage our hardware.

## Private laptop policy

*With the private laptop policy, Sytac will let you choose a laptop of your liking that will become your own after two years, meant for both personal and/or business use. Sytac will pay 50% of your laptop; the other 50% will be deducted from your net salary over 24 monthly down payments.*

- You can choose your laptop(s), not exceeding **€4.000** inc. VAT.
- Sytac will order the laptop and pay the amount at first in full.
- Over the following two years, **50%** of the laptop price is deducted from your monthly net salary.
- If you leave Sytac within one year from purchase, you must pay back the total amount (under deduction of already paid installments).
- If you leave Sytac before the two-year term, you must only pay back the rest of your down payments.
- If you leave Sytac unwillingly before the one-year term, you only have to repay the remaining installments.
- In any case, the laptop is considered your personal property after two years. Before these two years, you must keep the laptop in good condition, and any accessories, etc., are at your own cost.
- Only valid for employees.
- You may utilize this policy once assigned a project with a client or after completing your probationary period.

If you are interested in participating in one of the above programs, you have to email office@sytac.io with the details of the laptop and the link to the webshop with the price indication. Once the request is validated against your current project requirements, the purchase will be approved, and you will receive your new laptop shortly!

## Sytac loan laptop

The loan laptop policy is a laptop loaned to you by Sytac for the duration of your project, valid when working internally or with the client. The computer is free of charge and has to be returned at the end of your project (or employment) or can be renewed after three years.

- Sytac will provide you with a laptop with the following specs (or higher).
    - **15,6 inch - Intel I7 - 16GB RAM - 256GB SSD**
- You may loan this laptop for the duration of your project
- You are 100% responsible for keeping the laptop neat and in good working condition.
- You are not entitled to this policy when you receive a laptop from the client and/or join the private laptop policy.
- You must remove all data and perform a clean install of the OS before returning the laptop.
- In any case, the laptop is considered Sytac's property.
- Only valid for employees.

Due to government regulations, Sytac prohibits providing more expensive high-level laptops to employees. We understand that performance is essential, especially for software developers. Therefore, we introduced the Private Laptop Policy as described below.

## Client laptop

If you work on a project for one of our clients, there is a high probability that the customer will provide you with a laptop. While some clients require you to work on their machines, others allow you to bring a personal laptop. Unfortunately, Sytac has no control over the laptop regulations of the clients. If the client does not provide you with a computer, Sytac can provide you with a laptop for which we have the loan laptop and the employee laptop policy.
