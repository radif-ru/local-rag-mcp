# Hour Registration

Every outsourced employee, freelancer, or business partner working with or via Sytac have to register their hours on a monthly bases via the timesheet application. For this, we use an internally build an application that you can find here: http://timesheet.sytac.io/

**Access**

You can access the application with your Sytac Google credentials, which will be created for you by your manager on your hire date. If you are unable to log in for whatever reason, please contact your manager. 

**Deadline**

Submit your timesheet by the end of each calendar month, and no later than the 5th working day of the following month. If, for example, the last working day of the month is on a Thursday, the deadline for submitting your timesheet is on Thursday the seventh of the following month. You will receive a reminder on the first working day of each month, and another one on the 5th working day of the month.

**Creating a timesheet**

When you log in to the application you will see an overview page with “Your Timesheet”, on the right of the screen you see a button called “create new” after you are asked to create a month of the designated timesheet. 

**Filling out a timesheet**

Once you’ve created a timesheet, you will see 3 columns: client, leave, and internal. In the first column, you have to write the hours that you’ve worked on a project for the client. The client can be selected from the drop-down list. If your client is not on the list, please contact your manager.

The “leave” column will be automatically filled out for you based on the time-off registered in BambooHR. If your time-off in BambooHR does not match with your timesheet, you won’t be able to submit your timesheet. 

The last column is called “internal” which is meant for registering internal Sytac work if you are for example to work on an internal project. You’re not allowed to select or write internal hours without the approval of your manager. 

(**Optional) Request Signature**

Depending on the project you work on, your client manager might need to sign off on your timesheet. If this is the case, you’ll see this request pop-up under the client column. If this happens, please enter the email address of your client manager. He or she will receive an email with the request to sign your timesheet. You will be notified once your manager has approved the timesheet. 

(**Optional) Upload Attachement**

Depending on your project, you might need to upload an additional file. If this is the case, your manager will inform you which file is needed. In most cases, you’ll have to upload a screenshot of your approved hours in the client portal, or in other cases a manually signed timesheet from the client.
