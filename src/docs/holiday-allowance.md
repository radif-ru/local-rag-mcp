# Holiday Allowance

Each calendar year, you are entitled to a holiday allowance of 8%, which equals a thirteenth month's salary. If you have only been employed by the Sytac for part of the calendar year then this entitlement will be calculated on a pro-rata basis.

The holiday allowance is paid out simultaneously with the salary for the month of May. If you wish to have this paid out monthly, please contact *office@sytac.io* 

If your employment contract is terminated, then the holiday allowance will be calculated on a pro-rata basis. In that case, the holiday allowance will be paid out no later than the month following the month in which your employment contract is terminated.
