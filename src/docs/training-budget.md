# Training Budget

The training budget is a yearly allowance to cover the cost of personal development expenditures. The training budget does not cover the costs of buying hardware or software, which is covered in the [tech budget](https://www.notion.so/6a2b9bc29b2f4c46a4f0e7a5319b7f4b?pvs=21). 

The maximum cost per year can not exceed **€2.400** (excluding VAT). On rare occasions, an exception may be made. This is always in coordination with your Consultant Manager.

The training budget cannot be accumulated or carried over to the following year

## What is covered?

In general, all work-related training and adherent are covered by this training policy. To give you an example, the most common training request:

- Formal training sessions
- Employee coaching and mentoring
- Participating in conferences and workshops
- Online training courses or workshops
- Educational subscription
- Cost of examinations
- Books*

[Dutch lessons ](https://www.notion.so/Dutch-lessons-9cce5cd884ea48f6b6d12fcd537113ef?pvs=21)

If the training is abroad, please refer to this [page](https://www.notion.so/3032f671fe3b4edb9354a55a1dbd5934?pvs=21) for more details.

* Books can also be bought from the training budget. However, books purchased through the training budget remain property of Sytac and are expected to be returned to the DevHub after you finish reading. If you wish to own the books, you can purchase them through [the tech budget](https://www.notion.so/6a2b9bc29b2f4c46a4f0e7a5319b7f4b?pvs=21).

## **Training budget request procedure**

This procedure should be followed when employees want to request a budget for activities related to the training budget and **ONLY** after it was agreed on with the Chapter Lead:

1.  The employee makes a training budget request in BambooHR.
    1. Go to my info in [BambooHR](https://sytac.bamboohr.com/) and request a change
    2. Select training budget request
    3. Fill out the necessary information (effective date, type, description, cost, and the optional comment)
2. After receiving the approval confirmation through BambooHR, the employee can make all the reimbursement arrangements his or herself or ask the Consultant Manager to assist.
    1. ⚠️ All invoices must be addressed to the company name of your tribe: Sytac B.V, Sytac Digital BV, Sytac Development BV or Sytac Technologies BV.
    2. See [reimbursement](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21) on how to be reimbursed.

## **Repayment of training costs**

When the Company pays you to attend training courses, you agree that if your employment terminates after the Company has incurred liability for the cost of you doing so, you will be liable to repay some or all of the fees, expenses, and other costs:

- if you cease employment before you attend the training course, but the Company has already incurred liability for the Costs, 100% of the Costs or such proportion of the Costs that the Company cannot recover from the course provider shall be repaid;
- if you cease employment during the training course or within six months of completing the training course, 100% of the Costs shall be repaid.
- after six months of completing the training course, your ROI has been completed, and you are not obliged to repay any of the costs for that training course.
