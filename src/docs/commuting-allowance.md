# Commuting allowance

Though many of us work from home, sometimes, we need to travel to an office, and you are entitled to compensation for the travel cost. As a Sytac employee, you can choose between the following reimbursement options.

1. Kilometre allowance
2. NS Business card
3. Lease Car & Fuel Card
4. Bike Policy 

Remember that you can only use one of these reimbursement options. It is not possible to opt for multiple reimbursement options simultaneously. If you want to switch your travel reimbursement plan, please get in touch with the back office **(office@sytac.io)**

## 1. **Kilometre Allowance**

The standard option you’re automatically entitled to is the kilometre allowance. The allowance is €0.23 per kilometre travelled, whether by bike, train, car, or even when you walk. You can request your kilometre allowance via Moss - you can find the [reimbursement process](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21) here. by selecting “add mileage.” The number of KM is calculated automatically based on the fastest route between the destinations. You can’t adjust this.

## 2. **NS Business card**

An NS business card is an OV chip card billed directly to Sytac. You can use this card to travel in all forms of public transport and even rent bikes. Of course, this card can only be used for business-related travel, and you’re not allowed to use it on the weekends or holidays. If you want an NS Business card, please get in touch with the back office (**office@sytac.io)** and your Consultant Manager **(cm@sytac.io).**

**These are the rules for the NS business card:**

- The NS card may only be used for work-related commuting travel.
- You may not use the card on weekends or (public) holidays.
- A penalty fee of €20 per trip, plus the commute cost, is withheld from your salary if you use the card for personal reasons.
- You must return the card if you leave Sytac within a week of your last employment day.
- Report immediately to Sytac's back office when the NS card is no longer usable due to damage, stealing, or loss.
- You can only use the card for the 1st class section if the commuting takes at least 1 hour and you are allowed to work on the train (billable hours). This option requires **prior approval.** Please contact your consultant manager to discuss it.

## 3. Lease Car Policy

Opting for a lease car through Sytac gives you an opportunity for convenience and flexibility in daily commuting. Here are some rules that are good to know before you make a final decision.

### Contract Duration?

Upon choosing this option, you'll enter into a contract with our lease car provider for a duration of either 4 or 5 years. This allows you the comfort of driving a high-quality vehicle without the burdens associated with ownership.

### Monthly Cost?

A lease car is not provided without a charge. The monthly cost typically ranges between €500 and €1,000. This amount is directly deducted from your gross financial compensation. Additionally, a yearly tax, known as "addition" (or "bijtelling" in Dutch), is applicable. It stands at 16% of the car's value for fully electric cars and 22% for hybrid or fuel-powered vehicles. This tax is automatically deducted from your net salary each month. The beneficial aspect of our lease car policy is that all other associated expenses – insurance, road tax, regular maintenance, and even fuel or charging costs – are consolidated into the monthly lease price. As a result, the lease car offers significant convenience, reliability, and flexibility.

### Fuel Card Usage?

While the fuel card adheres to a fair use policy determined by your regular home-to-office commute, it can still be utilized for private trips over short distances. Please note, however, that refueling or charging the car outside the country is prohibited.

### Contract Extension?

As your lease car contract approaches its expiration, there is an option to extend it, but only for an additional 12 months.

### Waiting Period?

It's important to note that the average waiting time for obtaining a lease car can span between 9 to 12 months.

### Want to lease?

Should you have an interest in or questions about leasing a car through Sytac, please don't hesitate to get in touch with our team at [office@sytac.io](mailto:office@sytac.io).
