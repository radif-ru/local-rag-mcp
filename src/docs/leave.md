# Leave

### Introduction

We all work hard, and we recognize that! To give our best and live a well-balanced life, we need to take time off to pursue interests outside of work, whether that means sitting on a beach or climbing a mountain.

At Sytac, we believe you should take responsibility for managing your own time, communication, and collaboration with your client and on-site team. To that end, we’ve adopted a responsible vacation policy that gives each employee the flexibility to take planned vacations as needed. 

## Leave types

At Sytac, we have the following leave types: 

| [Vacation Days](https://www.notion.so/Vacation-Days-4eb23db0869e49238eb9663d67743771?pvs=21) | 20 annual leave days and 5 additional annual leave days |
| --- | --- |
| [Client Closing Leave](https://www.notion.so/35d83014afca41c7839423b64cb9fc15?pvs=21) | As of the third day, your client is closed, your leave only counts for 50%  |
| [Special Leave](https://www.notion.so/Special-Leave-3676ebe2830c49bb8f00dd9d3cad3479?pvs=21) | Additional time off for special circumstances |
| [Public Holidays](https://www.notion.so/Public-Holidays-3b35f823550440f4af3ec07afd356d5f?pvs=21) | National holidays on which the company and her clients are closed |
| [Sick Leave](https://www.notion.so/11e9edd96e67441aab67c752948a4b9f?pvs=21) | When you’re feeling under the weather |

## Requesting time off

You can request your time-off via BambooHR. Being professionals, we expect that you discuss your leave with both your client manager and team, before making the request via BambooHR. 

Generally speaking, a two-week notice for a couple of leave days, and a six-week notice for a couple of weeks is custom. If you plan to take more than 3 weeks off, we suggest that discuss this with your client manager at least 3 months in advance.
