# Special Leave

Regardless of the existing holiday arrangements, the employee will be granted additional leave in the case of:

- Posting the banns (giving notice of one’s intended marriage): a half-day
- The employee’s wedding: two days
- Wedding of parents, parents-in-law, foster parents, step-parents, brothers, sisters, brothers-in-law, sisters-in-law, children, foster children, or stepchildren: one day, provided that employee is attending the event.
- Spouse’s/partners childbirth: five days (in case of full-time employment). You can also apply for additional parental leave for partners.  Contact Back Office in case you want to apply. They can help you further.
- Death of a spouse and or one of the children, parents, or parents-in-law that are part of the family and or living with the employee: from the day of decease up to and including the day of the burial or cremation.
- Death of a child, parent, parent-in-law, foster parent, step-parent, grandparent, child-related by marriage, foster child, stepchild, grandchild, brother, sister, brother-in-law or sister-in-law not living with the employee: one day, plus the day on which the burial or cremation takes
place, unless the employee is not attending the event in question.
- The time needed to visit a doctor or specialist
- Moving house, provided the employee is changing his/her address and lives in a self-contained dwelling: one day
- 25-year, 40-year and 50-year wedding anniversary celebrations of the employee or his/her parents or his/her parents-in-law: one day.

There are two kinds of special leave:

1. Special leave paid 100% - for maternity/paternity leave, for moving, doctor appointments or different family occasions (wedding, funeral, anniversaries);
2. Special leave paid 70% - for additional paternity leave, parental leave or care leave.

To request special leave please send an email to office@sytac.io, notifying how many hours would you like to request and under which category (from the list above). 

The back office will add the requested amount of hours to your profile in Bamboo. Every case will be considered individually, so we can decide together if we release all available yearly hours or just the requested ones.

If you have any further questions, please, send them to office@sytac.io or contact directly our back office colleagues - Magda or Kirstin.
