# Holiday expiration and buy-back offer

In 2022 we want to give you the opportunity to sell your holiday hours. We remind you hereunder the rules:

**Expiration of unused holidays**

All full-time Sytac employees receive 160 official holiday hours and 40 additional holiday hours per year. Official holidays will expire after six months from the given year, and the additional holidays expire after five years. If you have more than 240 holiday hours at this moment, there is a risk that they will expire on June 30th, 2022 (click [here](https://www.rijksoverheid.nl/onderwerpen/vakantiedagen-en-vakantiegeld/vraag-en-antwoord/hoe-kan-ik-mijn-vakantiedagen-opnemen) for exact details, in dutch).

**Buy-back holiday hours**

Seen the unusual circumstances and risk of expiration holidays, we like to give you an offer to buy-back your unused holidays. You can find the compensation for this on your salary slip; on the right side, you see "uurloon" and multiply this by the number of hours you want to sell and add 8% holiday pay. As an employer, we can only buy-back the additional holiday hours, not the official 160-holiday hours. If you have less than 200 hours available, we can maximum compensate you for 40 hours (based on full-time ployment). If you have more than 200 hours available, we can reimburse you for 40 hours plus the additional hours above 200. The above consideration is based on full-time employment, part-time contracts are considered pro-rated.

The total amount will be paid out with your salary or, if you like, added to your tech budget. This offer is valid till June 30th, 2022.

If you have any questions or want to sell your additional holidays, please send an email to office@sytac.io
