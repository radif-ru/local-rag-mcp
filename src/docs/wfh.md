# Tech/WFH Budget

IT professionals need tools to perform their craft in the software development sector. We want our employees to have the ability to choose their preferred hardware tools to be as efficient and productive as possible.

Knowing that working from home or a hybrid system will be the new norm. We have decided to give you a budget of **€500** to prepare your workspace. This amount will be added to your tech budget on your starting date. In addition, you will receive a budget of **€30** per month.

This policy covers the following hardware products.

- Mouses and keyboards
- Cables, adapters, and converters
- Memory and storage devices
- Headsets and webcams
- Hardware repairs
- Phones, Tablets & Laptops
- Printer
- Software programs & subscriptions
- Books and e-books
- Working desks & office chairs
- Internet subscription (max €20,- per month)
- NEW! Since April 2025 treadmill compatible with desk

As part of the tech budget policy, we also approve work-related purchases not listed above as long as you use it at least 80% for business. 

## **How to request a tech budget?**

You have to request your tech budget via BambooHR. On the right side of the “My Info” section, you can find the button “Request a Change” and then select “Tech Budget Request.” The request will be sent to your consultant manager for approval.

Once your consultant manager has approved your request, you will receive a notification via BambooHR, and you can purchase the requested product or service. **⚠️ Please make sure that the invoice meets all the requirements for reimbursement.** For more information, please refer to the [reimbursement policy](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21).

| **Effective Date** | **The date you plan to make the purchase or have hosted the activity.** |
| --- | --- |
| Tech Type | The type of tech budget request that you’re making (e.g., Working desks) |
| Price | ⚠️ If you’re applying for an expenditure, enter the amount with a minus sign. You don’t have to do this if you request compensation for an additional tech budget. |
| Comment | A description of the product or service. |

## **Conditions**

Materials valued at less than €450 are the property of Sytac for 12 months, while materials exceeding €450 are the property of Sytac for five years. After these periods, the goods become the property of the employee. Should employment be terminated within the respective depreciation period, the employee must return the goods on their last working day, unless the employee opts to  "take over" the goods at their remaining book value. The depreciation is calculated as follows:

- Materials costing less than €450 are depreciated linearly over 12 months, at a rate of 8.33% per month.
- Materials costing €450 or more are depreciated linearly over 5 years, at a rate of 20% per year.

Additional earned budget will be paid out with your last salary payment. The unused base budget will be forfeited. Advances will be prorated and rebalanced to the number of applicable months.

- All materials should contribute to improving working conditions
- All costs should be advanced by the employee and [reimbursed](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21) via Moss
- All invoices should be addressed to the name of your tribe: Sytac BV, Sytac Digital BV, Sytac Development BV, or Sytac Technologies BV, Rozenstraat 1, 2011LS Haarlem
- Only additionally earned tech budget can be paid out with a salary payment
- We do not accept tech budget requests once you’ve announced your resignation.
