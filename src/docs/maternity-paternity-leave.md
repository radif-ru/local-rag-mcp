# Maternity & Paternity leave

This policy applies to all company's permanent, full-time, or part-time employees. Employees with temporary/short-term contracts may use this policy at their manager’s discretion. This policy doesn’t cover supplementary employees like contractors or ZZPers.

This policy goes into effect as of August 1st of 2022 and according to the Dutch labor law is calculated based on the **maximum daily salary of €290,67 (2025).** 

- Women are entitled to **6 weeks of birth leave (geboorteverlof)** and **10 weeks of maternity leave**. These 16 weeks will be reimbursed 100% of the base salary (the maximum daily salary limit does not apply to birth and maternity leave).
The first 6 + 4 weeks should be taken consecutively. The remaining four weeks can be used flexible over 30 weeks.
- Partners are entitled to **1 week of birth leave** **(geboorteverlof)** and **5 weeks of paternity leave**. The first week will be reimbursed at 100% pay, and the five remaining weeks will be reimbursed at 70% (with the salary capped at the maximum daily salary). These weeks should be taken within six months after birth.
- Both parents are entitled to **9 additional weeks of parental leave (ouderschapsverlof)** at 70% pay (also capped at the maximum daily salary). These weeks should be taken within the first year after birth.
- Both parents are always allowed to take less leave.

## **How do I request m**aternity & paternity leave**?**

- You can request maternity & paternity leave via BambooHR as [special leave](https://www.notion.so/3676ebe2830c49bb8f00dd9d3cad3479?pvs=21).
- Birth leave does not need prior notice and may be requested on stating the date but needs to be taken within the first four weeks after birth. You do need to inform your Manager of the starting date.
- Your Consultant Manager and the project manager must approve both maternity & paternity leave at least four weeks before the leave. To proceed with the request, please, send an email to **cm@sytac.io**.

## Additional Option

If you decide not to use the nine additional weeks of parents’ leave, Sytac will reimburse you for five weeks of maternity leave at 100%, plus an additional month's salary. 

If you choose this option and terminate your contract 12 months after birth, you must repay the additional income. 

## Conditions

When referring to salary, we refer to a base salary without bonuses or other compensations. If you work part-time, the periods mentioned above are calculated pro rata.
