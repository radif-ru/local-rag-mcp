# **Client closing days**

Many of our clients observe mandatory days off or have specific closing periods throughout the year. To harmonize our operations with client practices and offer flexibility to our consultants, the following policy has been instituted:

**Mandatory Days Off:**

For the first two days of any year that your client is closed, you are mandated to take those days off.

**Special 50% Cost Vacation Offer:**

If your client is closed for more than two days in a year, you're entitled to our special holiday benefit. Starting from the third day of client closure, for every additional day, you can request in Bamboo HR four vacation hours combined with 4 [Sytac Program Days hours](https://www.notion.so/e463d065f8a34f8795e678aeb0a2c968?pvs=21), effectively costing you only half of a regular vacation day.

**Ensuring Service Continuity:**

Our primary goal is to ensure that we provide continuous support and address our client's needs efficiently, even during their closing periods. Closing schedules can vary significantly among clients, across departments, and even within teams. Additionally, clients often request exceptions to continue receiving services during their closing times. Therefore, if your client has a closing period, it's essential to confirm specific availability conditions. 

Please adhere to the following steps:

1. **Consultation:** Discuss and confirm your availability with your Client Manager during the client's holiday period.
2. **Access the Document:** Download or open the **Work Exception Document** in your browser.
    
    [Client Approval Form for Employee Availability During the Closing Period.pdf](https://prod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/cddd36b4-3f5b-4295-90b0-42e28b9a8a65/0d192fe0-fe47-473b-a818-2dfb9212b6b4/Client_Approval_Form_for_Employee_Availability_During_the_Closing_Period.pdf)
    
3. **Client Approval:** Fill out the document in collaboration with your client's manager. 
4. **Submission:** Once filled and signed, send the Work Exception Document to [office@sytac.io](mailto:office@sytac.io).

**Support & Queries:**

Should you have questions or require more clarity on this policy, please get in touch with your Consultant Manager or the back-office team. We're always here to assist!
