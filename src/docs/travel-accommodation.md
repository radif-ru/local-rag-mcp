# Travel & Accommodation

Sytac considers three types of travel (Business trips, Personal development, and Client request). You can find further details of each type below. 

## **Business Trips**

For all trips (that are Sytac's initiative) related to visiting the project offices, Sytac covers the following costs:

- Train & flight tickets
- Hotel: Sytac will cover every night during the visit, including one night before and one night after. The hotel should be within walking distance of the project offices.
- Meals: Sytac will reimburse you up to 50 EUR per day (including tips) for meals. A copy of your itemized meal receipt will be required for reimbursement, as we need to be able to see what was purchased. We do not refund alcoholic beverages.
- Transport: Sytac will cover the travel from your home to the airport and from the airport to the hotel. Please consider public transportation and shuttle services, where possible, as an alternative to taxi travel.

Both flight tickets and hotel will be purchased directly by Sytac, please contact your consultant manager to arrange all the details.

For expenses related to Meals and Transport, you have to request the reimbursements directly, please follow the standard procedure stated [here](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21).

## **Personal development**

For all trips related to personal development, such as technical training or participation in conferences and workshops, expenses will be deducted from the training budget under the following conditions:

- Train & Flight tickets - up to 300€ round trip (If the cost of the tickets exceeds this value, please contact your consultant manager for approval.)
- Hotel: up to 150€ per night (If the night's cost exceeds this value, please contact your consultant manager for approval.) You can use the budget to cover every night during the event, including one night before and one night after.
- Meals: 50 EUR per day (including tips) for meals. A copy of your itemized meal receipt will be required for reimbursement, as we need to be able to see what was purchased. We do not refund alcoholic beverages.
- Transport: Travel from your home to the airport and from the airport to the hotel. Please consider public transportation and shuttle services, where possible, as an alternative to taxi travel.

*Before joining any activity, you must request your **Consultant Manager’s approval.** Please follow the procedure stated [here.](https://www.notion.so/4f0d6c109b984bb9aadebff79af951ca?pvs=21)* 

For this type of travel, you must organize and purchase everything and request the related reimbursements. For refunds, please follow the standard procedure stated [here](https://www.notion.so/c9bca5937bfe471fb2c7944d95b23b0f?pvs=21).

## **Client request**

When the trip is at the project manager's request, the client's conditions apply. Contact your project manager to discuss the details.
