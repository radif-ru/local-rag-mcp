# Vacation Days

Vacation days are paid time off work granted by employers to employees to be used for whatever the employee wishes. There are two forms and one exception.

## 1. Annual Leave

Each employee is entitled to 20 days per year for each calendar year worked, based on 40 hours worked per week. If you have only been employed for part of a calendar year or part-time, then this entitlement will be calculated pro-rata. Annual leave days expire six months into the following year. 

## 2. Additional Annual Leave

In addition to the annual leave, Sytac grants the employees an additional five days per year for each calendar year worked, based on 40 hours per week. These additional annual leave days expire five years after the year they were granted. 

## Mandatory Leave Days

Mandatory leave days are two days per year for you to take a vacation day. These days are determined by your client and limited to the first two days that your client is closed. If your client is closed for more than two days per year, please refer to the [client closing policy.](https://www.notion.so/09a552e4295b49a595d10e6033ca94ca?pvs=21) 

## Holiday expiration and buy-back offer

We want to allow you to sell your holiday hours. We remind you hereunder of the rules:

### **Expiration of unused holidays**

All full-time Sytac employees receive 160 official holiday hours and 40 additional holiday hours per year. Official holidays will expire after six months from the given year, and the additional holidays expire after five years. If you have more than 240 holiday hours now, there is a risk that they will expire on June 30, 2023 (click [here](https://www.rijksoverheid.nl/onderwerpen/vakantiedagen-en-vakantiegeld/vraag-en-antwoord/hoe-kan-ik-mijn-vakantiedagen-opnemen) for exact details in Dutch).

### **Buy-back holiday hours**

Seen the unusual circumstances and risk of expiration holidays, we would like to offer you to buy back your unused holidays. You can find this compensation on your salary slip; on the right side, you see "uurloon" and multiply this by the number of hours you want to sell and add 8% holiday pay. We can only buy back the additional holiday hours, not the official 160 holiday hours, as an employer. If you have less than 200 hours available, we can compensate you for 40 hours (based on full-time ployment).

If you have more than 200 hours available, we can reimburse you for 40 hours plus the additional hours above 200. The above consideration is based on full-time employment, and part-time contracts are considered pro-rated.

The total amount will be paid out with your salary or, if you like, added to your tech budget. This offer is valid till June 30, 2022.

If you have any questions or want to sell your additional holidays, please send an email to [office@sytac.io](mailto:office@sytac.io)
