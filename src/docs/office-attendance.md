# Office attendance

*This policy outlines the office attendance requirements for all full- and part-time employees. Considering both periods when they are assigned to client projects and when they are not. The aim is to ensure continuous collaboration, knowledge sharing, and professional development, while also providing the flexibility to meet client needs and preferences.*

**Client Project Attendance**
When you're on a client project, the office attendance arrangements will be made in collaboration with the client's team, taking into account your preferences. This could range from being primarily on-site at the client's location, a mix of client site and remote work, or entirely remote, depending on what works best for the project and the client's preferences.

**Between Projects Attendance**
Between client projects, we ask that you spend a minimum of three days per week working from either our Amsterdam or Haarlem office. This helps us stay closely connected with you and ensures a smooth process in matching you with new projects, with the aim of getting you back to client work as soon as possible. Ultimately, this benefits both you and Sytac as we work together toward success! The specific days you're in the office can be flexible and decided through discussions with your consultant manager. This allows for accommodating personal commitments and optimizing team collaboration.

**Notification**
If, for any reason, you can't meet the attendance requirements, whether set by the client or the company, please inform your consultant manager or the client team lead in advance and provide a valid reason. We'll consider exceptions on a case-by-case basis to ensure flexibility.

**Review**
This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure its relevance and effectiveness, taking into account feedback from employees, client requirements, and industry best practices.
