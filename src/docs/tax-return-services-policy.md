# **Tax Return Services Policy**

## **Service Description:**

Sytac provides professional tax return services (via our accountant) to assist individuals with their tax declarations. The service includes thorough assistance in completing tax declarations, addressing queries, and ensuring compliance with tax regulations. 

## **Service Requirements:**

To proceed with a tax declaration you should possess a DigiD to log in to the tax authorities' website.
You’re also required to provide all necessary documentation and information relevant to your tax declaration.

## **Service Rates:**

€49,00 per tax declaration (2024)
€89,00 per hour rate for more complex tax questions beyond the scope of standard tax declaration assistance (2024)

## **Service Dates and Locations:**

- 18th of March 2024: Haarlem office
- 26th of March 2024: Amsterdam

April: Additional dates may be available based on demand.

## **Booking Procedure:**

You can book your 30 min slot by office per mail (office@sytac.io).

Slots are available on 18th of March from 10:00 to 17:00 every 30 min and on the 26th of March from 10:00 to 19:00 every 30 min. Please, always come with an appointment, so we can avoid people waiting for their turn.

Appointments are scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis.

Upon booking confirmation, you will receive further instructions regarding the preparation of documents and information required for the tax declaration process.
