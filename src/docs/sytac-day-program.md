# Sytac Day Program

## What is it?

The **Sytac Days** is a program designed to **give you time away from your client** in order to attend to your Professional Development needs and to participate in Sytac activities. In other words, this program **allows for paid time-off** to complete an eligible activity.

▶ This program covers a **specific set of activities**. See “[Eligible Activities](https://www.notion.so/Sytac-Day-Program-e463d065f8a34f8795e678aeb0a2c968?pvs=21)” section below.

⚠️ The **Sytac Days Program** effectively replaces the Training Days policy previously in place.

## Do I qualify?

This program is **only available** for consultants ****that are **fully contracted** at the client.

⚠️ For all other cases please consult with your Chapter Lead to discuss a more personalized plan.

### For example:

I am an employee with a **40 hours** contract at **Sytac** and I work **40 hours** at the **client** ⇒  I automatically **qualify**

I am an employee with a **40 hours** contract at **Sytac** and I work **36 hours** at the **client** ⇒  Consult with your **Chapter Lead**

## Eligible Activities

Below is a list of eligible Sytac Days activities:

- Training and Development
    - Formal training programs leading to a certificate and Corporate training programs
    - Does NOT include self-study courses
    - Attending conferences
    - Taking an exam
- Chapter, DevJam (workshops, etc), and Nibbles & Drinks contributions
- DevJam Blog contributions are NOT covered in this program
- Contributing to Internal Projects

## How can I request a Sytac Day?

1. Get the informal approval by discussing with your Chapter Lead and Consultant Manager
2. Make the request via [BambooHR](https://sytac.bamboohr.com/). 

## How many Sytac Days can I take per year?

In theory, there is no maximum number of Sytac Days allowed per year.

## Budget

The expenses incurred while performing Sytac Days-related activities might be eligible for a refund. Please check the [training budget](https://www.notion.so/Training-Budget-4f0d6c109b984bb9aadebff79af951ca?pvs=21) to see if your type of expense qualifies for reimbursement.
